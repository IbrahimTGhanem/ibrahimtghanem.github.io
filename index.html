I am a PhD student in quantitative geography at the University of Toronto Mississauga. I study walkability, mobility, and health outcomes across neighbourhoods. I use GIS, spatial statistics, and mixed methods to measure access, behaviour, and equity.

I collaborate with community partners to map perceived walkability and collect survey data. I focus on Arab communities in the Toronto CMA and on underserved areas citywide. I aim to produce open, reproducible analysis and tools.

Outside research, I enjoy cycling and building small web tools for mapping. See my projects and code on GitHub.
